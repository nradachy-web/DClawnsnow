Our Story

Built on Hard Work, Grown by Reputation

I started DC Lawn and Snow in 2006 after working alongside family for three years. When the opportunity came to step out on my own, I took it — beginning with just 15 accounts and a commitment to do things right.

By 2012, word of mouth had grown our customer base to around 60, and I brought on help to keep up with demand. Today, we proudly serve over 170 lawn and snow customers, including large commercial properties and multi-acre residential estates on our weekly schedule.

Our growth and exceptional customer retention come down to one thing: putting quality work above everything.
